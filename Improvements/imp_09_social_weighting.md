# Improvement Area 9: Social Weighting & Sentiment Proxies

## The Objective
To enable Hexamind to distinguish between "Technical Truth" and "Social Truth" by extracting community sentiment (forums, Reddit, social media views) as a weighted variable in the final report.

## Why this is necessary
The Gemini report identifies that the "Green Bubble" (social stigma) is a more powerful driver than "60Hz refresh rate" (technical spec). Hexamind's current agents are "Spec-Heavy." They analyze the hardware but ignore the **Cultural Pressure** metadata.

## Maximization Strategy: "The Vibe Check"

### 1. Sentiment Proxy Retrieval
The `Researcher` will be given specialized "Sentiment Queries":
- "Reddit consensus on [Topic]"
- "[Topic] user complaints vs praise threads"
- "Social stigma associated with [Competitor]"

### 2. Weighting the "Halo Effect"
The `Analyst` will incorporate a **"Brand Equity / Halo Score."** If a product has a 90% "sentiment dominance," technical failures are automatically downgraded in their predicted impact on market performance.

### 3. Sociological Context Inversion
The final report will include a section on **"The Social Landscape,"** explaining how public perception overrules technical reality, just as Gemini did with the iMessage ecosystem.

## Expected Outcome
Reports that understand "The Human Component." Instead of just saying "Option A is technically better," the AI can say "Option A is better, but Option B will win because of the social 'Halo Effect'."
