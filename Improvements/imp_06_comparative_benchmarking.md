# Improvement Area 6: Comparative Benchmarking (Entity-vs-Entity)

## The Objective
To force Hexamind into a "Comparative Mindset," ensuring that every report automatically identifies the primary rival of the subject and performs a side-by-side benchmark of critical metrics.

## Why this is necessary
The Gemini report is essentially a "Shadow Comparison" of Apple vs. Samsung and Mac vs. PC. It doesn't analyze Apple in a vacuum. It uses the competitor's stats (Samsung's 77% retention) as the "Baseline of Reality."

A report without a competitor benchmark is just a brochure.

## Maximization Strategy: "Auto-Rivalry Logic"

### 1. Rival Identification Node
Update the `Orchestrator` to automatically perform a "Competitive Landscape" search in the first 30 seconds of execution. 
- *Task:* "Identify the top 3 rivals to [Subject] in terms of market share and technical parity."

### 2. Side-by-Side Tables
The `Analyst` agent will be mandated to produce a **"Competitive Parity Table"** for the conclusion.
- Rows: Price, Core Feature X, User Retention, Future Pipeline.
- Columns: Subject vs. Rival 1 vs. Rival 2.

### 3. The "Stance Map"
Introduce a feature called the **"Stance Map"** which shows if the subject is "Ahead," "Par" or "Behind" the industry average for a specific KPI (Key Performance Indicator).

## Expected Outcome
Instant contextualization. The user won't just know that the subject is "good"; they will know exactly where the subject is beating (or losing to) its competitors.
