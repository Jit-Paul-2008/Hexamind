# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction

ARIA ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. **If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Registration**: Name, email address, password
- **Profile Data**: Research preferences, saved queries, saved reports
- **Communications**: Messages, feedback, support requests
- **Uploads**: Documents, files for analysis

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent, features used
- **IP Address**: For security and analytics purposes
- **Cookies**: To improve your experience (non-tracking)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Web Research**: Summary data from web sources (via Tavily API)
- **Model Outputs**: Inferences and analysis results

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
- Process your requests and handle customer service
- Personalize your experience
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and enhance security
- Send service updates and announcements
- Conduct research and analytics

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

- **Local Development**: SQLite database (your machine)
- **Production**: PostgreSQL (encrypted at rest)
- **Retention**: User data retained while account is active
- **Deletion**: Upon request, we delete your account data within 30 days

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- Password hashing using bcrypt
- Regular security audits
- No storage of plain-text passwords
- Rate limiting and DDoS protection

**However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.**

## 6. Sharing Your Information

We do **not** sell your data. We may share information:
- With service providers (Tavily for web search, Ollama for AI)
- When required by law or regulation
- To protect our legal rights and prevent fraud
- With your explicit consent

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- **Access**: Obtain a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Opt-Out**: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **privacy@aria.local**

## 8. Cookies

We use cookies for:
- Session management (non-functional cookies)
- User preferences
- **No tracking cookies** — we do not track you across websites

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

## 9. Third-Party Services

Our Services integrate with:
- **Tavily**: Web search API (see https://tavily.com/privacy)
- **Ollama**: Local AI models (no external data sharing)
- **Sarvam**: Language transforms (optional, future)

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our Services are **not intended for users under 18**. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn we've collected data from a child, we will delete it within 30 days.

## 11. International Users

If you are outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the US. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer.
